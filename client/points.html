Stat points:

Stat points are upgradeable points that grant improvements to your base stats.

You start off with 2 stat points in every category, leaving you only 3 more points to spend. If you need to spend more than 3 points to get the build you want, you must subtract points from other categories

Name Description
Speed Increases your movement speed
FOV Increases your field of view
Attack Speed Increases the rate at which you can hit
Resistance Decreases the damage you take
Struc. Dmg Increases your damage to structures
Player Dmg Increases your damage to players
Build Limit Lets you get more of certain structures (totem and landmine are the exception)
Vanity "Look ma, no points!" This stat does absolutely nothing, but maxing it out lets you choose your character color
