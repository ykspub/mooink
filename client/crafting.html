Crafting & Inventory

Inventory

General info

* Open inventory by clicking "inventory" in top right
* Beige colored slots are actionbar slots, which correspond to the actionbar on the bottom, the blue colored slots are your inventory slots
* Drag items around to move them
"Box Item", "Split Half", "Split 5", etc are special slots. Drag an item to that slot to use it.
What these slots do should be pretty obvious. "Split 5" has a dropdown menu where you can change it to split any multiple of 5.
"Box Item" will turn your stack of items into a box. If you have a stack of 10 wood, box item will make it such that you have a single box, containing 10 wood.
It is advisable to always use boxes when crafting. "Box Item" will unbox an item if a box is dragged inside.

Crafting

* First, locate the "Crafter T.1" object in your inventory, and drag it to an actionbar slot. Then, place the crafter down on the map
* Put your crafting inputs onto the crafter (box them only if possible), and hit the crafter once with a melee weapon

Crafting recipies

Multiplying all the crafting inputs will also multiply the output you get. It is recommended you box your items first before putting them in the crafter (because you can't fit 20 wood around a crafter)
Note that a box can only contain one type of item. Breaking a box will return the mats back to you, but breaking an unboxed structure will not
